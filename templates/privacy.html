Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Your Privacy Matters - RahaSoft is committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. This policy explains our privacy practices in detail.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

Personal Data:
  • Full name and contact information
  • Email address and phone number
  • Job title and role within your organization
  • Password (encrypted and hashed)
  • Profile picture (optional)

1.2 Business Data

As an ERP platform, RahaSoft processes business-related information you input, including:

  • Employee records and HR information
  • Inventory and product data
  • Financial transactions and accounting records
  • Customer and supplier information
  • Business analytics and reports
  • Document attachments and files

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our service:

  • Login timestamps and session duration
  • Feature usage and navigation patterns
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and location data (city/country level)
  • Performance metrics and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Process and store your business data
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Facilitate team collaboration and communication
  • Backup and synchronize your data across devices

2.2 Communication

  • Send system notifications and alerts
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Share important service updates and security notices
  • Send billing and payment confirmations

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Conduct security monitoring and threat detection

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

RahaSoft never sells, rents, or trades your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing Scenarios

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate our platform (hosting, email, analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with advance notice)
  • Your Consent: With your explicit permission for specific purposes

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Business Data: Retained according to your data retention settings
  • Usage Logs: Typically retained for 12 months
  • Deleted Data: Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request

6. Your Rights and Controls

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")

7. Cookies and Tracking

RahaSoft uses cookies and similar technologies to:

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

9. Children's Privacy

RahaSoft is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware of such data collection, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post updates on our website with the revision date
  • Give you the opportunity to review changes before they take effect
